About Focus Tax Accountant

Your trusted accounting partner in Perth, WA

At Focus Tax Accountant, we believe great accounting is more than just compliance — it's a strategic partnership. With decades of experience serving businesses across multiple industries, we understand the unique challenges Australian businesses face.
